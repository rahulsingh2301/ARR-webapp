Consulting and Advisory

Today's companies require an accountant to perform various roles in the available bandwidth of the office, however the accountant may not have subject matter expertise and we offer such services for the company under our consulting bandwagon.

The offerings of the consulting and advisory services are:

  • Performing Due-Diligence and Pre-Due-Diligence for companies looking to raise funds.
  • Business Valuation and Valuation Reports for FEMA/Companies Act.
  • Equity and Debt Syndication.
  • Succession Planning and Family Office Advisory.
  • Balance Sheet Resizing and Restructuring.
  • Advise on setting up of Holding and Subsidiary companies.
  • Cross Border Transaction Advisory.
  • International Taxation.
